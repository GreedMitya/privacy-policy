# Privacy Policy — Albion Potion Calculator

_Last updated: April 24, 2026_

This Privacy Policy describes how the **Albion Potion Calculator** mobile application ("the App") handles your information. Our primary goal is to provide a useful tool while maintaining the highest standards of user privacy.

## 1. Data Collection and Usage

**Albion Potion Calculator does not collect, store, or share any personal information.**

The App is designed to run entirely on your local device. We do not access your contacts, location, camera, or any other sensitive personal data. All your crafting preferences, favorite items, and settings are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to our servers.

## 2. Network and External Services

### Market Data Integration
To provide real-time crafting calculations, the App fetches public market data from the **Albion Online Data Project API** (https://www.albion-online-data.com). These requests are anonymous and do not contain any personal or device-identifiable information.

### Google Play Billing
The App uses the **Google Play Billing Library** to process premium feature purchases. 
- **Financial Privacy**: All payment processing is handled securely by Google Play. We do not have access to, nor do we store, your credit card numbers, bank details, or other payment credentials.
- **Entitlement Tracking**: We receive a unique Transaction ID from Google to verify your purchase and enable Premium functionality.

## 3. Advertisements

**The App displays third-party banner advertisements.**

We use third-party advertising services to serve these ads. These services may collect and use information about your device, such as your Advertising ID, IP address, and usage data, to provide relevant advertisements and measure ad performance. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of our advertising partners for more details on their data practices.

## 4. No Third-Party Analytics

The App does **not** include third-party analytics or tracking services (such as Firebase Analytics or Google Analytics). We do not track your behavior or usage patterns within the App.
