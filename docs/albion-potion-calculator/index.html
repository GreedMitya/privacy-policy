"We respect your privacy as much as your crafting profits. Our policy is built on the foundation of absolute data transparency and zero unauthorized collection."

1. Data Collection & Privacy

The **Albion Potion Calculator** has been developed with a "Zero-Tracking" architecture. We do **not collect, process, or transmit** any personally identifiable information (PII).

Local Storage

Settings and favorites are stored only on your physical device. We have no cloud access to your local data.

No Permissions

We do not request access to your contacts, photos, camera, or location services.

2. Google Play Billing

Our Premium features are integrated with the **Google Play Billing Library**. To ensure your security, all payment operations are offloaded to Google’s encrypted systems.

  • Financial Isolation: We do not have access to your bank details or credit card information. Google handles the complete payment lifecycle.
  • Entitlement Verification: Upon payment, Google provides us with a unique Transaction ID used solely to unlock and verify Premium features on your account.

3. Advertisement Disclosure

While the current version of the Albion Potion Calculator is **ad-free**, the developer reserves the right to introduce third-party advertisement integrations in future updates.

If advertisements are introduced, this policy will be updated to include links to the respective ad-service privacy policies and to disclose any data (such as Advertising IDs) used to serve relevant content.

4. Analytics & Tracking

Unlike many modern applications, we do **not** utilize third-party analytics services (such as Firebase, Meta Pixel, or Mixpanel). Your usage habits within the App remain private and are not monitored by us or any external agencies.
